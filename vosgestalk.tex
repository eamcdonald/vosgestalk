\makebeamertitle

\begin{frame}{Introduction}
    In this talk I will discuss the tangent groupoid theory of pseudodifferential operators whose symbols have asymptotic expansion like
    \[
        \sigma(x,\xi) \sim \sum_{\ell=0}^k \sum_{j=0}^{\infty} a_{j,k}(x,\xi)\log(|\xi|)^\ell
    \]
    as $\xi\to \infty,$ where $a_{j,k}(x,\xi)$ is homogeneous of order $m-j$ in $\xi.$
\end{frame}
